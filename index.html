As a warm and welcoming community devoted to academic excellence anchored in Jewish values, The Epstein School ignites intellectual curiosity, fosters compassion and integrity, nurtures creativity, and inspires confidence in each student to lead a life of purpose.

Our graduates advance to the top schools in Atlanta, where they are appreciated not only for what they know, but how they think and who they are.
