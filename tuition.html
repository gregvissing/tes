A Jewish Day School Education Lasts a Lifetime

Investing in your child’s education today will set them on a path to success in high school, college, and beyond. We know the investment in independent school education requires thoughtful financial planning and we are committed to helping you navigate the process and consider the tuition payment options we have as well as the tuition reduction programs we offer.

You will find that the Epstein community is more than a school, it is truly a family. We encourage and support one another and we have a shared value of educating the whole child. An Epstein education adds depth to your child’s learning and strengthens their individual character. Throughout their Epstein journey, we are shaping, molding, and helping our students understand what it means to be a mensch. Our graduates are confident, lifelong learners who are curious, critical thinkers, and compassionate, thoughtful young adults who are grounded by their unique Jewish identity.
