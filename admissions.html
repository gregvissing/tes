The Strongest Foundation

At The Epstein School, we offer a curriculum that is at the cutting-edge of academic innovation. Our program inspires students to explore, discover, debate, and imagine—the very skills that are essential for becoming original thinkers and principled leaders. All within a warm, nurturing environment with a small teacher-to-student ratio where individual attention to each child’s needs provides a springboard for success. Here, our preschool through middle school curriculum provides the core foundation for students to grow and master the skills needed to succeed in high school and beyond. We invite you to experience Epstein through a personal tour.
